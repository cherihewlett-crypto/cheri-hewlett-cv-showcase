## Best-fit recommendation for Cheri

For a technical leadership / AI systems audience, the strongest sequence is:

1. **Leadership concierge README** (Rank 1 pattern) as the front door  
2. **Structured resume backend** (Rank 4 pattern) as the source of truth  
3. **Selective thought-leadership layer** (Rank 2 pattern, scoped down) for durable authority

This gives high innovation signal without overbuilding v1.
